20th Century Photos

Why 20th Century Photos?

We chose to add 20th century photos to our website due to the importance the camera had in this era of turmoil and action. From conflicts such as two world wars to the achievements of important figures such as MLK and Einstein.

But Why these Photos?

We chose these photos due to the significance of them, from showcasing how people enjoyed their time a century ago, to how we treated people just several decades ago. They all show some sort of historical reference, with some being famous photos, such as the migrant mother.
