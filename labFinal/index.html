What are Cursed Photos?

Cursed photos are images that portray a scene that often is mysterious, unsettling, or abnormal. They leave much of what is happening to the viewers imagination, as they are often taken with little to no context. Sometimes they are pictures of everyday activites with something strange sticking out, or something that looks like someone's fever dream. They typically are taken in poor quality or content, while those in the photo seemingly do not notice anything wrong.

Where did it Start?

The concept initially started on Tumblr in 2015 from the account named cursedimages. The first image posted was a picture taken of a farmer surrounded by crates of tomatos. The seemingly mundane everyday activity has been given an air of strangeness due to the camera quality and the zero context given.
